TRIBUTE TO KONIDELA KALYAN BABU

Power Star Pawan Kalyan, whose real name is Konidela Kalyan Babu,Pawan Kalyan was born on September 2, 1971, in Bapatla, Andhra Pradesh, India. He is the younger brother of noted actor Chiranjeevi and comes from a family deeply rooted in the film industry

About KONIDELA KALYAN BABU

Pawan Kalyan made his acting debut with the 1996 Telugu film "Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi." However, it was his role in the 1998 film "Tholi Prema" that catapulted him to stardom. The film not only became a huge success but also won him a National Film Award for Best Feature Film in Telugu. Throughout his career, he delivered several memorable performances in movies like "Gabbar Singh," "Attarintiki Daredi," "Jalsa," and "Kushi." His charismatic screen presence and unique style endeared him to fans of all ages Pawan Kalyan's entry into politics marked a significant chapter in his life. He founded the Jana Sena Party in 2014, with a vision to bring about positive change in the political landscape of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. He has been vocal about issues affecting the common man and has strived to make a difference through his political career.
